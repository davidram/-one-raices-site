The Buyer's Guide

Everything you need to know before buying your next home, step by step.

1 Get pre-approved first

Before you fall in love with a home, talk to a lender. A pre-approval letter tells you your real budget and makes your offer far more competitive when you find the right place.

2 Define your must-haves vs. nice-to-haves

List your non-negotiables (location, number of bedrooms, school district) separately from things you'd like but could live without. This keeps your search focused and your decisions fast.

3 Tour with a critical eye

Look past staging — check water pressure, open and close windows, look at the roof age, and ask about the neighborhood at different times of day if you can.

4 Make a strong, smart offer

We'll pull comparable sales so your offer is competitive without overpaying, and help you decide which contingencies matter most to keep versus which you can safely waive.

5 Inspection & closing

A home inspection protects you from costly surprises. We'll walk you through negotiating repairs, finalizing your loan, and what to expect on closing day.
